About

This site can be used to track what ever type of data that you want and See your progress as time goes on. The inspiration for this page comes from me constantly asking the questions "How often does this happen?" or "how much better have I gotten at X".

On this page you will find a general run down on how the site works

What are Habits and Subhabits?

Habits are meant to represent the main categories for the items that an individual would want to track. These are meant to be more broad if there are smaller subcategories that will also be tracked. Subhabits are these smaller categories. A habit can have any number of subhabits all with unique names but two subhabits cannot share the same name.

What are the types of logs?

Currently there are logs for numeric and written data. Numeric logs handle data that involes recording numbers and are saved in decimal form. Written logs are more so keep track of notes.

There will be support for more types of data form and record keeping in the future with more customizable data logs.

Can I share data with someone else?

Yes! If you have their username then you can use the 'Add Viewer' form at the bottom of a habit or subhabit to share it. If you add them to a subhabit then they will be able to view only the data in the subhabit but if they are added to a habit then they will be able to view both habit and all associated subhabit data.

How can we contact you?

If you run into any bugs or are having problems with the app, then feel free to send me an email at kevyneherrera@gmail.com. I am also open to hearing suggestions on how to imporve the site.
